We Are Micro Shield

Company Protects People from Broken Glass

Description :

This company strives to provide innovative solutions to protect people from the dangers of shattered glass. This is achieved by applying a gelatinous layer to the surface of the glass, either from the front or from both sides. When the glass breaks, this layer holds it in place and prevents it from shattering and scattering, ensuring the safety of people in the area .

Features of using the gelatinous layer :

Protection from injuries : The gelatinous layer prevents the glass from shattering and scattering, reducing the risk of people being injured .

Easy cleaning : Broken glass is easier to clean when it is held in place by the gelatinous layer .

Low cost : The technique of using a gelatinous layer to protect glass is an economical solution compared to other protection methods .

Variety : Different types of gelatinous layers are available to meet the different needs of users .

These types include :

Protective layers : Focus on preventing glass shattering and scattering .

Thermal insulation layers : Help regulate room temperature by blocking sunlight .

Colored layers : Add an aesthetic touch to the glass .

Glossy layers : Reflect light and add a stylish appearance .

Classified layers : Meet international safety standards .

Benefits :

Family protection : The gelatinous layer provides additional protection for young children and the elderly from the dangers of broken glass .

Peace of mind : Using the gelatinous layer gives you peace of mind from worrying about the dangers of broken glass .

Protecting property : The gelatinous layer prevents damage to furniture and furnishings when the glass breaks .
